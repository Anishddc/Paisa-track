About Me

My mission and the vision behind Paisa Track

Our Story

Paisa Track was born out of a simple need - to help people manage their finances without compromising on privacy. In today's world where financial data is constantly shared and sold, I wanted to create an app that puts users first.

Founded in 2023, Paisa Track started as a personal project when I was frustrated with the available financial tracking solutions. Most apps required cloud sync, had subscription fees, or lacked important features.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to take control of their finances through intuitive, private, and feature-rich tools that respect user data and privacy.

Our Approach

I believe in:

  • Privacy First: All your financial data stays on your device. I don't collect, store, or analyze your personal information.
  • Simplicity: Financial management should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial literacy level.
  • Complete Control: You should have full ownership and control over your financial data.
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no unclear data policies.
