Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Paisa Track

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, Paisa Track stores all data locally on your device. We don't use cloud storage or send your financial information to any servers. You can also enable biometric authentication for added security.

Is Paisa Track free to use?

Paisa Track is completely free during the beta phase. We plan to maintain a free tier with all essential features after full release, with optional premium features available.

Can I export my financial data?

Yes, Paisa Track allows you to export your data in PDF and CSV formats for your records or for use in other applications.

Which currencies are supported?

Paisa Track supports all major global currencies with proper formatting and conversion capabilities.

How can I backup my data?

You can create local backups of your data within the app. These can be restored if you change devices or reinstall the app.

How do I download the beta version?

You can download the beta version directly from our Mega link provided on this website. The app is currently not available on app stores during the beta testing phase.
