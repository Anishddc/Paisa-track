Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Paisa Track is designed with privacy as its foundation. All your financial data stays on your device, and I collect minimal information.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Paisa Track ("I", "me", or "my") handles your information when you use my mobile application. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand what data I collect and how I use it.

Information I Don't Collect

It's important to understand that Paisa Track is built as a local-only application, which means:

  • I do not collect your financial data (transactions, accounts, balances, etc.)
  • I do not have access to your personal financial information
  • I do not store your data on my servers
  • I do not track your usage patterns within the app
  • I do not share any of your information with third parties

Information I May Collect

While the app itself doesn't transmit your financial data, I may collect limited information in the following scenarios:

Crash Reports

If you opt in to sending crash reports, I may receive technical information about your device to help me fix problems, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • No personal or financial data is included in crash reports

App Store Information

If you download the app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store, those platforms may collect standard information according to their own privacy policies.

Voluntary Communication

If you contact me via email or through my website for support, I will have access to the information you share in your communications.

Data Storage

All your financial data in Paisa Track is stored:

  • Locally on your device only
  • Encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Never transmitted to my servers or third-party services

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can export your data at any time in various formats
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or using the app's data clearing function
  • You can create and restore local backups

Children's Privacy

Paisa Track is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe I have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact me so I can promptly remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update my Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
