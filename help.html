Getting Started

How to Install Paisa Track

Learn how to download and install Paisa Track on your device.

  1. Visit the Download section of our website
  2. Click on the "Download Beta" button to download from our Mega link
  3. Open the downloaded APK file on your device
  4. You may need to allow installation from unknown sources in your device settings
  5. Follow the on-screen installation instructions
  6. Open the app once installation is complete

Note: The current version is in beta and is only available through direct download, not through app stores.

Setting Up Your Profile

Configure your personal settings when you first launch Paisa Track.

  1. Launch the app after installation
  2. Enter your name when prompted
  3. Select your preferred currency
  4. Choose whether to enable optional features like biometric authentication

Tip: You can always change these settings later in the app settings.

Creating Your First Account

Add your first financial account to start tracking your finances.

  1. Go to the Accounts section
  2. Tap the "+" button to add a new account
  3. Select the account type (Bank, Cash, Digital Wallet)
  4. Enter a name for the account
  5. Enter the current balance
  6. Save the account

Understanding the Dashboard

Learn how to navigate and interpret the main dashboard of Paisa Track.

  • Top Section: Shows your total balance across all accounts
  • Income & Expenses: Displays your current month's financial overview
  • Recent Transactions: Shows your latest financial activities
  • Quick Access: Provides shortcuts to frequently used features

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe with Paisa Track?

Yes, all your financial data stays on your device. Paisa Track doesn't store your financial information on any servers or cloud services.

How do I create a backup of my data?

Go to Settings > Data > Backup. From there, you can create a local backup file that can be restored if needed.

Can I sync my data across multiple devices?

Since Paisa Track is focused on privacy and local data storage, automatic syncing isn't available. However, you can manually export your data from one device and import it on another.
